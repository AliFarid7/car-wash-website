About

FUE Car Care center is a well organized, large station, providing a vast range of auto maintenance services under one roof, in the New Cairo district. It pulses with the capacity of servicing more than 15 vehicles at a particular time within normal service time other than auto repairs. We believe every customer is a valuable business partner. FUE Car Care center is totally committed to ensuring best services and products are perfectly synchronized to meet diverse customer needs.

With the objective of fulfilling the long awaited need of loyal customers FUE Car Care center celebrates our presence in New Cairo where we have been very successful in operating over the years and plan to further expand the network. As such the overall position of FUE Car Care has been further strengthened as Total Auto Care Specialists. FUE Car Care center team loves vehicles and always gives maximum service to our clients.
